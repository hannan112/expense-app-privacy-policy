Account & Data Deletion Request

At ResellTrack, we value your privacy and give you full control over your data. If you wish to delete your account and all associated data, you can do so directly within the app or by using the request method below.

How to delete in-app

1. Open the ResellTrack app.
2. Navigate to the Profile screen.
3. Scroll down to the Danger Zone section.
4. Tap Delete Account & Data and confirm.

Web Request Method

If you have already uninstalled the app and wish to request data deletion, please email us from the email account associated with your ResellTrack profile.

Request Deletion via Email

What information is deleted?

Upon processing your request, the following data will be permanently removed from our servers:

  • Your user profile and settings.
  • All recorded Trips and GPS route history.
  • All recorded Expenses and links to receipt images.
  • Your login credentials and authentication record.

Please note: Deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. Data removal is typically processed within 24-48 hours.
